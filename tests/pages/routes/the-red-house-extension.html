Description

Start same as The Red House, but move up past pockets via sidepull/crimps and top out approx. 7 feet above the original finish. Don't be afraid to throw for the final jug (you're not coming off it). The extension makes the problem way better IMO.

Location

Super Mario boulder

Protection

A couple crash pads and a spotter or two
