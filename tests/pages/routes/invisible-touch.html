Description

Quality. Only negative is that for this problem to be climbed the river must be either frozen or at very low water. Start on flat jug. Move through a pinch and then ride the crimps on the arete. Finish with tough moves on slopers.

REMEMBER:
-If it has rained in the past 24 hours, do not climb at Eldon.
-Wipe your shoes off before you climb.
-Leave no trace. Erase tick marks. Pack out any trash, yours or others'.

Location

Far right side of the River Boulder, right above the water.

Protection

3-4 pads. Bring a tarp if the ice/snow is melting. Clean top off before hand. If there is ice and the sun is out, the ice will melt, making the problem unclimbable. If it is winter, it is best to come a day early and shovel and remove ice.
